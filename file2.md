# DESIGN SYSTEM

## Theme 1 — Cinematic Dark

```
Background
#080808

Surface
#121212

Primary Gold
#C9A84C

Secondary Gold
#E3C56F

Text
#FFFFFF

Secondary Text
#B5B5B5

Borders
rgba(201,168,76,.2)

Accent Glow
rgba(201,168,76,.35)
```

**Visual style**

* Matte black backgrounds with subtle film grain.
* Gold accents reserved for CTAs, icons, and highlights.
* Glassmorphism used sparingly with soft blur.
* Cinematic gradients and vignette overlays.
* Smooth page transitions resembling film fades.
* Premium serif headings (e.g., Canela/Cormorant Garamond) paired with a clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Geist).

# Animation & Interaction Guidelines

* **Opening sequence:** cinematic logo reveal with film projector sound (optional).
* **Page transitions:** smooth cross-fades and subtle zooms.
* **Hero:** parallax video with layered text animation.
* **Cards:** lift, glow, and slight 3D tilt on hover.
* **Gallery:** masonry layout with lightbox for images and videos.
* **Showreels & Lessons:** YouTube-inspired cards with duration, play button, and metadata.
* **Scrolling:** section reveals, counters, marquee client logos, and horizontal cinematic sliders.
* **Micro-interactions:** magnetic buttons, animated underline navigation, and elegant loading states.

The result is a production website that feels less like a corporate portfolio and more like an immersive digital film studio—professional for clients, inspiring for students, and visually memorable for every visitor.
